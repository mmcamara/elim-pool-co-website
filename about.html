Meet the Founder

Mauricio Camara

After a successful 15-year career in IT and programming, Mauricio swapped his keyboard for a pool pole with a specific mission: to elevate the standard of service in South Florida.

Seeing a need for greater reliability and professionalism in the local market, Mauricio founded Elim Pool Co. to bring a truly worry-free experience to homeowners. He applies the same precision and troubleshooting skills from his tech background to managing your pool's water chemistry and equipment — ensuring your water is always safe, balanced, and protected.

The name Elim comes from a place of rest and refreshing waters — a reminder of what great service should feel like. Mauricio's goal is simple: show up on time, communicate clearly, and care for every pool as if it were his own.

Beyond the pool deck, Mauricio is a dedicated husband and father of two daughters, an active volunteer at his church, a bass player, and an appreciator of fine watches — a testament to his love for precision and timing.
