Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At Elim Pool Co., we stand behind the quality of our work. If you are not satisfied with a service visit, please contact us within 48 hours and we will make it right.

Service Refunds

If a scheduled service visit was not completed due to an error on our part, you will receive a credit toward your next invoice. We do not offer cash refunds for completed service visits; however, if a service was performed incorrectly or incompletely, we will return to remedy the issue at no additional charge.

Equipment & Repair Refunds

Parts and materials purchased on your behalf for repairs are non-refundable once installed. If a part fails within 30 days of installation due to a defect, we will replace it at no additional labor cost. Manufacturer warranties on parts apply as provided.

Prepaid Services

If you have prepaid for services and wish to cancel, unused portions will be refunded on a pro-rated basis, less any administrative fee of up to $25.

How to Request a Refund or Credit

Contact us directly within 48 hours of the service in question:

  • Phone: (954) 361-6292
  • Email: mauricio@elimpool.co

We will review your request and respond within 2 business days.
